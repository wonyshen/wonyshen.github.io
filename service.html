Pricing Examples

Each video is unique, so it's challenging to set rates without understanding the project's specific goals. I've spent thousands on just a few seconds of footage, while other times, a 3-minute video costs only a few hundred dollars. As a baseline, if you need someone to capture 4K footage without any pre-planning, the rate is $100 per hour with a 2-hour minimum.
